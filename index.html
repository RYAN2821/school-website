RESIDENTIAL FACILITIES

Techno College provides campus facilities for boys. Our campus comes equipped with air-conditioned rooms. We aim to create a comfortable living environment that is similar to a second home for all students. The children are always under the care of our team of considerate wardens and staff. We’ve created multi-cuisine and balanced meal plans that promote healthy eating habits. We also provide our students with the opportunity to explore the outer world within timeframes. Strict hostel times are ensured for the security of all students. Students can explore the nearby areas with our Sunday outings program. Students are provided basic health-care facilities at the hostels and if required, further care is extended by a reputed hospital. Additionally, sports activities are also available inside the hostel campus for students to enjoy during their off time or on the weekends. Indoor games like carrom and multimedia options are available for the same purposes.
